# Fundraising Collaboration Platform
_Connecting placement agents to maximize capital raising potential._

---

## 🧭 Context
This project involved the management and technical delivery of a secure, collaborative network for Placement Agents. The goal was to move away from competitive, siloed fundraising models and create a "trusted network" where agents could share mandates to optimize capital raising while maintaining strict ownership of their proprietary professional relationships.

---

## 🧩 Problem
- **Sub-optimal Capital Raising**: Fundraising opportunities were historically limited by the individual reach of a single agent rather than the collective power of a network.
- **Competitive Silos**: Existing networks lacked a structured framework for collaboration, leading to a "zero-sum" mindset.
- **Relationship Risk**: Agents were hesitant to share deal information due to the risk of losing ownership or recognition of their unique investor relationships.

---

## 💼 My Role
- **Roadmap & Sprint Planning**: Adopted a Feature-First Phasing strategy, prioritizing core connectivity (Invite Systems and Opportunity Flows) to ensure a stable MVP before layering on branding modules.
- **Stakeholder Demos**: Led comprehensive internal and client facing demos upon the successful completion of each epic, keeping the project aligned with the client’s original vision.
- **Change Control Management**: Managed scope creep through strategic trade-off negotiations. When new requirements emerged, I worked with stakeholders to identify and postpone lower priority features to maintain the delivery timeline.
  
---

## 🧠 Solution & Approach
3-tiered federated architecture designed to balance transparency with privacy
- Super Admin (Platform Tier): Provided global oversight of all networks, agents, and opportunities, with centralized control over platform-wide branding and settings.
- Network Admin (Federated Tier): Empowered network owners to manage their own branded environments, invite agent firms, and monitor engagement through an activity dashboard.
- Agent Flow (Operational Tier): Enabled firm-level users to list and respond to opportunities, featuring integrated security like 2FA and granular internal permissions (Admin vs. Standard).

---

## 📊 Impact / Results
- **Secure Collaboration**: Successfully deployed a platform that technically enforced relationship privacy for 100% of participating firms while enabling mandate sharing.
- **Execution Discipline**: Maintained a high bar for "Definition of Done" by utilizing a strict validation process for all Acceptance Criteria, resulting in on-time delivery of major epics.
- **Omnichannel Transparency**: Provided Network Admins with real-time visibility into opportunity engagement, reducing manual reporting overhead across the network. 

---

## 🔍 Learnings
- Transparency is a key driver of trust in competitive ecosystems.  
- Fine-tuned access control can maintain both cooperation and privacy.
