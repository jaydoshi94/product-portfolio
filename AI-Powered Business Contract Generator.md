# AI Assisted Mental Health Platform
_Bridging the gap between reactive therapy and proactive, 24/7 emotional support._

---

## 🧭 Context
This project involved the development of an AI-driven mental health companion designed to provide immediate accessibility and early behavioral intervention. By leveraging advanced conversational models and optimized prompt logic, the platform provided users with a "first-line" support tool available 24/7 while monitoring sentiment trends to detect early signs of mental health decline.

--- 

## 🧩 Problem
- **The Accessibility Gap**: Traditional therapy is often restricted by high costs and long wait times, leaving users without professional support during non-business hours or moments of immediate crisis.

- **Reactive Care Models**: Mental health treatment is frequently reactive, occurring only after a significant crisis has manifested, rather than identifying subtle shifts in well-being early enough for preventive care.

- **Data Fragmentation**: A lack of continuous mood tracking makes it difficult for users to recognize patterns or triggers between scheduled sessions.

---

## 💼 My Role
I defined the product roadmap, owned the sprint board, defined feature prioritization, collaborated with the developers & SMEs, and supervised design to delivery phases.

---

## 🧠 Solution & Approach
- **Dynamic Chat**: A persistent, "long-memory" conversational flow that allows users to vent and interact indefinitely, maintaining context over time.
- **Structured Exercises**: A goal-oriented module featuring pre-defined steps and a definitive start/end, providing users with actionable, evidence-based coping tools.
- **Intelligent Risk Scoring**: Implemented an automated calculation engine that analyzes interactions to generate a "Risk Score," providing administrators with a real-time health-monitoring dashboard.

---  

## 🔍 Learnings
- Learned the importance of "long-memory" system design understanding that a user’s mental health trajectory is best understood through continuous patterns rather than isolated data points.
- Developed a nuanced understanding of how to design structured vs. unstructured features based on real-time user state and psychological need.
- Gained deep experience in managing the delicate balance between maintaining a "natural" conversational flow and enforcing strict safety protocols through advanced prompt engineering.
